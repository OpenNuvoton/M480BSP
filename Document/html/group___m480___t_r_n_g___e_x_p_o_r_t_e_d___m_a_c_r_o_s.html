◆ TRNG_SET_CLKP

#define TRNG_SET_CLKP (   clkpsc)

Let TRNG engine know the currrent PCLK frequency. The CLKPSC is the peripheral clock frequency range for the selected value , the CLKPSC setting must be higher than or equal to the actual peripheral clock frequency (for correct random generation).

Parameters
clkpsc0: PCLK is 80~100 MHz 1: PCLK is 60~80 MHz 2: PCLK is 50~60 MHz 3: PCLK is 40~50 MHz 4: PCLK is 30~40 MHz 5: PCLK is 25~30 MHz 6: PCLK is 20~25 MHz 7: PCLK is 15~20 MHz 8: PCLK is 12~15 MHz 9: PCLK is 9~12 MHz
Returns
None
